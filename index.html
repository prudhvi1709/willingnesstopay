Identify customer willingness to pay from call transcripts?

In debt collection calls, identifying payment intent is crucial. Collection agents need to assess if debtors are:

  • The right point of contact
  • Acknowledging the debt
  • Willing to pay
  • Making specific payment promises
  • Requesting payment assistance
  • At risk of default

Willingness to Pay streamlines this assessment. We analyze call transcripts to identify key payment indicators and supporting evidence.

This helps collection agencies, credit card managers, and financial institutions improve recovery rates and prioritize follow-ups.

Willingness to Pay uses:

  • Private LLMs. Transcripts stay in the Straive tenant with no model training on your data.
  • JSON schema validation to ensure consistent, structured analysis.
  • Ground-truthing by extracting verbatim conversation snippets as evidence.

The interactive dashboard lets you analyze multiple transcripts, with detailed reasoning for each assessment and keyboard navigation to review results efficiently.
